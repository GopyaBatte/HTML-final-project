VISA related FAQ's

What is a Visa?

A visa is basically a travel document that plays a major role in helping you either enter or leave a country. A visa is mandatory to enter a country legally.

Will I need a visa to travel?

As said, the importance of visa should not be neglected. Some countries offer Visa on Arrival, while for some countries you are needed to carry a travel visa to enter the country legally.

Is there any cost for visa processing?

An Indian citizen has the benefit from a number of travel arrangements with countries from all over, and the fees might be waived off. In some cases, an individual will have to pay for the visa.

What is Visa on Arrival?

As the name suggests, is a term used when visas are issued when you enter the respective country. When a country offers visa on arrival, you do not need to apply for your visa before you fly.

Which countries offer Visa on Arrival?

Countries like Kenya, Thailand, Mauritius, Maldives, Cambodia, Nepal, Laos, Madagascar and so on offer Visa on Arrival.

How many days does it take for visa processing?

Visa process varies from one country to another. While some visas are issued the same day, some countries take longer to issue visas. It is recommended that you check for the specific information on the respective country’s embassy website.
